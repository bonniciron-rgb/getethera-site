FAQ

Questions service businesses ask before choosing Ethera.

What is Ethera?

Ethera is a WhatsApp-first CRM and operations platform for service businesses. It connects lead capture, inbox follow-up, bookings, billing, renewals and campaign reporting.

Who is Ethera built for?

Ethera is built for service businesses such as language schools, clinics, salons, gyms, tutors, tourism providers, driving schools and local operators that rely on WhatsApp conversations.

Does Ethera replace HubSpot?

Not always. Ethera can operate as the WhatsApp-first front office and sync qualified leads or deals into HubSpot when a business already uses HubSpot as its central CRM.

How are payments and invoicing different?

Stripe helps collect payments, deposits and subscriptions. InvoiceXpress helps create compliant invoices and receipts. Ethera connects both parts of the customer journey.

Can Ethera track marketing campaigns?

Yes. Ethera can track enquiries from Click-to-WhatsApp ads, QR codes, website CTAs, referrals, Instagram, Facebook and other customer entry points.
