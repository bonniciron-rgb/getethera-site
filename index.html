FAQ

Questions service businesses ask before choosing Ethera.

What is Ethera?

Ethera Growth Studio is a three-studio growth solution for service businesses. It combines Lead Gen + Marketing Studio, CRM Studio and Strategy Studio. SEO optimization belongs inside the marketing studio.

Who is Ethera built for?

Ethera is built for service businesses such as language schools, clinics, salons, gyms, tutors, tourism providers, driving schools and local operators that rely on WhatsApp conversations.

What is Strategy Studio?

Strategy Studio is the prescriptive layer. It reads lead generation, marketing, CRM and SEO signals, creates strategy snapshots and weekly plays, then measures whether those actions worked.

Where does SEO optimization fit?

SEO optimization is part of Lead Gen + Marketing Studio. It connects Google Business Profile foundations, review operations, profile health, posts and website actions to the enquiries and bookings they create.

Can Ethera track marketing campaigns?

Yes. Ethera can track enquiries from Click-to-WhatsApp ads, QR codes, website CTAs, referrals, Instagram, Facebook and other customer entry points.
