What it solves

No more lost context between enquiry, follow-up, billing and payment.

CRM Studio keeps the conversation, source, owner, booking, invoice, payment status and renewal record together, so the team always knows what happened and what must happen next.

WhatsApp CRM

Route direct messages, ad enquiries, QR leads, website CTAs and handoff requests into one inbox.

Bot intake

Use structured scripts to collect name, need, intent, preferred time and missing details before handoff.

Pipeline

Move leads through new, contacted, qualified, booked, won, lost and renewal stages.

Bookings

Connect appointments, trials, classes or consultations to the customer record and campaign source.

Billing and invoicing

Connect invoicing tools so confirmed bookings or contracts can trigger invoice creation and customer records stay current.

Payments

Track deposits, payment links, subscriptions, payment status and receipts beside the customer timeline.

Dunning

Spot failed, late or missing payments and trigger structured reminder workflows before revenue leaks.

Renewals

Keep contracts, recurring services and renewal moments visible so the team can act before customers lapse.

Team handoff

Assign ownership and preserve the full history when marketing leads need human follow-up.

Workflow

Capture, qualify, book, bill, collect, renew.

Step 1

Message arrives

Ethera captures source, message and contact details from WhatsApp.

Step 2

Bot qualifies

The script asks only the questions needed to prepare the next action.

Step 3

Team converts

Sales or reception handles the lead with context, owner and booking timeline.

Step 4

Billing runs

Invoice and payment workflows connect through the tools the business already uses.

Step 5

Dunning protects revenue

Late or failed payments can trigger reminders and follow-up tasks.

Step 6

Renewals stay visible

Recurring customers, renewals and revenue connect back to the original demand source.
