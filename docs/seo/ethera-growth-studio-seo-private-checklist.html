Google Search Console Checklist

  1. Confirm the correct property is being used: getethera.io.
  2. Submit the sitemap: https://getethera.io/sitemap.xml.
  3. Inspect the homepage and request indexing if Google has not crawled it.
  4. Inspect the three English studio pages and three Portuguese studio pages.
  5. Confirm Google-selected canonicals match the submitted canonical URLs.
  6. Check whether hreflang is accepted for EN and PT versions.
  7. Monitor impressions for branded queries: Ethera, Ethera Growth Studio, getethera.
  8. Monitor non-branded impressions for CRM, WhatsApp CRM, lead attribution and marketing analytics queries.
  9. If pages remain discovered but not indexed after 48-72 hours, strengthen internal links and resubmit priority URLs.

Do Not Reintroduce

  • Do not describe the full product as Ethera CRM; CRM is only one studio.
  • Do not make SEO Optimization a standalone pillar; it belongs in Lead Gen + Marketing Studio.
  • Do not show internal deployment/GitHub/Vercel readiness copy on the public homepage.
  • Do not let Strategy Studio Beta sound like a finished predictive AI product before roadmap data exists.
  • Do not overfill the homepage with every feature; detailed content belongs on studio or use-case pages.
  • Do not make unsupported claims about integrations until confirmed by the product roadmap.
